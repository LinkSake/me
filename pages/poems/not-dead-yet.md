## not dead yet

Hit, but not defeated  
Scared, but not wounded  
As the sun falls and set  
We too shall rest  

Let the night eat the light  
Let the rain fall and the wolves cry  
Because as the sun rises  
We too shall get on our feet again  

We are not dead, yet.
